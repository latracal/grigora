Mobile Friendly

The number of mobile devices worldwide is growing at an exponential rate. Thus, we need to ensure that our websites are mobile-friendly to avoid potential customer loss due to a bad mobile interface. Grigora is already optimized for mobile devices and passes all mobile-friendly tests.

Schemas help search engines to identify the type of content in your website and elements in each sections. Grigora adds a basic blog schema markup to make sure your website is compliant with schema.org guidelines.

Breadcrumb is often used by search engines to understand where the page sits on your website. Grigora provides option to add breadcrumbs along with its markups.
